Q1 Percent Change · Speed

Your Speed was 6 in Season 1. You're changing it to 9 for Season 2. What is the percent change in your Speed allocation?

%
Full Solution
1.Formula: (New − Old) ÷ Old × 100
2.Plug in: (9 − 6) ÷ 6 × 100
3.Simplify numerator: 3 ÷ 6 × 100
4.Divide: 0.5 × 100
5.Answer: 50%
Q2 Slope-Intercept · Decay

Your decay equation is y = −21x + 100. What does the slope tell you?

Explanation
Slope = rate of change. m = −21 means the bot loses 21 percentage points of power every minute it operates. The negative sign shows it's decreasing over time.
Q3 Distance Formula

A = (4, 2). G = (8, 2). What is the distance from A to G?

units
Full Solution
1.Formula: d = √((x₂−x₁)² + (y₂−y₁)²)
2.Plug in: √((8−4)² + (2−2)²)
3.Evaluate: √(4² + 0²)
4.Simplify: √(16 + 0) = √16
5.Answer: 4 units
Q4 x-Intercept · Decay Planning

E = 8. At what minute does your bot hit 0% power? Round to 1 decimal place.

min
Full Solution
1.Decay rate: (20 − E) × 1.5 = (20 − 8) × 1.5 = 12 × 1.5 = 18 per min
2.Equation: P(t) = 100 − 18t
3.Set to zero: 0 = 100 − 18t
4.Solve: 18t = 100 → t = 100 ÷ 18 ≈ 5.6 min
Q5 Unit Rate · Speed

A bot travels 240 cm in 5 seconds at constant Speed. What is the unit rate in cm/second?

cm/s
Full Solution
1.Formula: Unit rate = total distance ÷ time
2.Plug in: 240 ÷ 5
3.Answer: 48 cm/s
