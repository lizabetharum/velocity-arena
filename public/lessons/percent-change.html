Watch out: Always divide by the Season 1 value — not Season 2. The starting value is the reference point.
Special cases
Season 1 value is zero? Write "N/A." You can't divide by zero. That's a mathematical rule, not a mistake.
Same values both seasons? Write "0%." Zero percent change is a correct, complete answer — it just means that stat didn't move.

Turn the decimal into a percentage
0.5 × 100 = 50
Multiplying by 100 converts the decimal into a percentage. 0.5 = 50%. Always the last step.

Is Percent Change Proportional?
If a stat goes up 50%, do your results go up 50% too? It depends on the formula.
If Speed goes up 50%...
do wins go up 50%?
The answer depends on which stat changed and how the formula works.
✓ Proportional
Speed
4
→
6
(+50%)
Motor power S1 20%
Motor power S2 30%
Output also up +50%
The formula is linear. Double the stat, double the output. That's proportional.
✗ NOT Proportional
Power
4
→
6
(+50%)
Charge S1 (4²) 16
Charge S2 (6²) 36
Output up +125% — not 50%!
The formula is quadratic (P²). Small stat change = big output jump. That's NOT proportional.
