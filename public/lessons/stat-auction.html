The 4 Auction Rules
Your facilitator will read these out loud. Follow along.
Rule 1
One Package Only
Each team can win only one auction package today. Win one → you're done bidding.
Rule 2
Stay at 20
Your total stats must always add up to 20. If you gain points somewhere, remove them from somewhere else.
Rule 3
Use Them or Lose Them
Tokens don't carry over. You have 3 tokens (or 4 if your team is in last place). Spend them now.
Rule 4
Bids Are Final
Once you hold up your tokens and win — no take-backs. The package is yours.
Strategy tip: Before bidding opens, decide which stat your team needs most. Look at your residuals from yesterday — which formula gave you the worst result?

How to bid correctly
1
Your facilitator holds up the package card and reads it out loud.
2
Decide how many tokens you want to bid. Hold them in your closed fist — don't show anyone yet.
3
On the signal "Bid" — everyone opens their hand at the same time.
4
The team with the most tokens wins. Ties = coin flip.
5
Winners hand over their tokens and update their stat sheet right away before the next package opens.

Example of a good bid

"We bid 2 tokens for Speed +1. Our stat goes from 8 to 9. New motor power = (9 × 100) ÷ 20 = 45%."

ALL 4 FORMULAS AT A GLANCE
Speed motorPower% = (S × 100) ÷ 20
Double the stat → double the speed. Proportional.
Endurance P(t) = 100 − (20−E) × 1.5 × t
Power drops over time. Linear decay. t = time elapsed.
Turning arcSegments = T
turnRatio = 0.2 + (T × 0.02)
How sharp can you turn? Higher T = more arc control.
Power charge = P²
followThrough = P × 200
Double the stat → quadruple the charge. Quadratic.
