Systems of Inequalities

DAY 11

The Rules of the Arena

Before you can build any valid loadout, your configuration must satisfy all three constraints at the same time. Miss even one — and your robot doesn't clear the starting gate.

1
Speed > 5
You need at least 6 Speed points for a valid loadout. A Speed of 5 or lower disqualifies your robot.
2
Endurance > 3
You need at least 4 Endurance points. An Endurance of 3 or lower means your robot runs out of energy mid-match.
3
S + E + T + P = 20
All four stats — Speed, Endurance, Turning, Power — must add to exactly 20. No more, no less. (Each stat must be at least 1.)

Key Concept

A system of inequalities is a set of conditions that must ALL be true at the same time. Satisfying two out of three doesn't count — partial satisfaction doesn't qualify your robot.
