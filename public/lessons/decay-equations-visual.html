From Formula to Equation

Your P(t) formula is already linear — let's write it in slope-intercept form.

The Endurance Decay Formula
P(t) = 100 − (20 − E) × 1.5 × t
P(t) = motor power %  ·  t = time in minutes  ·  E = your Endurance stat

Find the X-Intercept

When does your bot hit the 20% power floor?

In Velocity Arena, power never drops below 20% — the game engine holds it there. So the real question is: when does P(t) hit 20? That's our x-interceptX-interceptWhere the line meets a threshold — here, the moment P(t) reaches 20%, the in-game power floor. After this point, power stays locked at 20%. for this lesson. If that moment comes before the 3-minute match ends, your bot is stuck at minimum power. If it comes after, your bot finishes strong.

Solving for t when P(t) = 20 (the power floor)
20 = m · t + 100
−80 = m · t (subtract 100 from both sides → 20 − 100 = −80)
t = −80 / m (divide by m — m is negative, so t is positive!)
t = 80 / ((20 − E) × 1.5)
Worked Example · E = 6
m = −(20 − 6) × 1.5 = −14 × 1.5 = −21
t = 80 / ((20 − 6) × 1.5) = 80 / (14 × 1.5) = 80 / 21 ≈ 3.81 minutes
A bot with E = 6 hits the 20% power floor at 3.81 minutes — after the 3-minute match ends. It survives!
