Formula Reference
Speed → Motor Power % = (S × 100) ÷ 20
Endurance → Power at t=1 min = 100 − (20 − E) × 1.5
Turning → Arc Segments = T  ·  Turn Ratio = 0.2 + (T × 0.02)
Power → Charge Speed = P²  ·  Follow Through = P × 200

The Question
Speed = 8 uses 8 of your 20 points. That leaves 12 points for Power, Turning, and Endurance combined. For each loadout: redistribute those 12 points, then recalculate all outputs. The answer is not "we give up 12 points" — it names which stat takes the reduction and what happens to that output.
