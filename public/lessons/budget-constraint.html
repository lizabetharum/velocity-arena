Design Your Loadouts
20 points, four stats — each number you add somewhere must come from somewhere else
The Constraint
Speed + Power + Turning + Endurance = 20
Add 2 to Speed → remove 2 from somewhere else. Both sides always weigh the same.
Rules
  • Each loadout must use exactly 20 points — no more, no less.
  • Every stat must be at least 1 point.
  • None of your three loadouts can match any personal stat card exactly.

The Trade-Off
If Speed = 8, what are you giving up? Show every number.
The Question
Speed = 8 uses 8 of your 20 points. That leaves 12 points for Power, Turning, and Endurance combined. For each loadout: redistribute those 12 points, then recalculate all outputs. The answer is not "we give up 12 points" — it names which stat takes the reduction and what happens to that output.
