Section 1 · The Formula

Power Decay Over Time

Every bot loses power as the match wears on — unless your Endurance stat is high enough to slow the drain. This formula shows exactly how much power your bot has at any minute of a match.

P(t) = 100 − (20 − E) × 1.5 × t
Power at time t = Base power minus decay
⚡
Power Floor: 20%
The game engine never lets power drop below 20%. Once the formula would give a result below 20%, power stays locked at 20% for the rest of the match. Your bot never fully dies.
Variable Definitions
Variable Meaning Range
P(t) Bot power at minute t (% of full power) 20% – 100%
E Endurance allocation — stat points invested in stamina 1 – 20
t Minutes elapsed since match start 0 – 5 min
1.5 Decay constant — how fast the game environment drains power fixed

⚡ If the formula gives less than 20%, power is capped at 20% — that's the power floor.

Worked Example · E = 6

With E = 6, the decay rate each minute is:
(20 − 6) × 1.5 = 14 × 1.5 = 21% per minute

The 20% floor kicks in at: t = 80 ÷ 21 ≈ 3.81 min — after that, power stays locked at 20%.

t (min) Calculation P(t)
0 100 − 21 × 0 = 100 − 0 100%
1 100 − 21 × 1 = 100 − 21 79%
2 100 − 21 × 2 = 100 − 42 58%
3 100 − 21 × 3 = 100 − 63 37%
4 100 − 21 × 4 = 16 → ⚡ floor 20%
5 100 − 21 × 5 = −5 → ⚡ floor 20%

Rows highlighted in yellow hit the 20% floor — the formula result was below 20%, so the game engine locked power at 20%.
