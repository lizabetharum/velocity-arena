Section 3 — Trend Line
Calculate the Slope (m)
The slope tells you how many extra goals your opponent scores for every 1 point increase in Speed.
m = (G₂ − G₁) ÷ (S₂ − S₁)
Slope Formula — Rise over Run
The slope (m) tells you the rate of change — how much the Goals number goes up or down for every 1 point increase in Speed. A slope of 0.75 means: every time Speed goes up by 1, they score 0.75 more goals on average.

Find the Y-Intercept (b)
b is the predicted Goals when Speed equals zero — the point where your trend line crosses the y-axis. Think of it as the “baseline” score before Speed adds anything.
b = G₁ − m × S₁
Y-Intercept Formula — use your slope m and Match 1 values

Section 4 — Loadout Strategy
Your Constraint & the Threat
Every bot loadout has one rule that can never be broken. You also need to name what makes your opponent dangerous.
S + E + T + P = 20
The constraint — your four stats must always sum to exactly 20
The Four Stats & Bot Formulas
These are the same formulas on your team reference card. They tell you exactly what each stat does to your bot.
Speed
motorPower% = (S × 100) ÷ 20
Every point of Speed adds the same amount of motor power. Unit rate
Endurance
P(t) = 100 − (20−E) × 1.5 × t
Power drops over time. At 3 min: P(3) = 100 − (20−E) × 4.5 Linear decay
Turning
arcSegments = T    turnRatio = 0.2 + (T × 0.02)
How sharp can you turn? Higher T = tighter arc. Linear
Power
charge = P²    followThrough = P × 200ms
Double the stat → quadruple the charge. Quadratic
Name the Threat
Look at your opponent’s stats. What is their highest stat? What does that mean for how they play? What do you need to counter?

Build Three Loadout Options
Create 3 different stat combinations for your bot. Each one must add up to exactly 20. Try different strategies — aggressive, defensive, balanced.
What Do These Strategies Mean?
Aggressive
High Speed and Power. Attack fast, charge hard. Wins if you can score before they do. Weak in endurance.
Defensive
High Endurance and Turning. Block drives, outlast the opponent, stay in position late in the match.
Balanced
No single weak stat. Adaptable across all situations. Trades peak performance for consistency.
