Endurance controls how long your bot stays strong. Low endurance = bot slows down faster. High endurance = stays at full power longer.
P(t) = 100 − (20−E) × 1.5 × t

Turning NUMBER REQUIRED
High Turning = tight sharp turns → your bot stays concentrated in a small area (good for guarding).
Low Turning = wide sweeping arcs → your bot covers more ground (good for hunting).
arcSegments = T  ·  turnRatio = 0.2 + (T × 0.02)

Power NUMBER REQUIRED
Power controls how hard your bot charges and pushes. It's squared — so doubling your power stat quadruples your charge.
charge = P²  ·  followThrough = P × 200 ms

Speed Formula
motorPower% = (S × 100) ÷ 20
How fast your bot moves. Higher speed stat = higher motor power = reaches the ball first.
Endurance — Power Over Time
P(t) = 100 − (20−E) × 1.5 × t
How much power your bot has left at minute t. Low endurance = drops faster.
Turning — Arc & Ratio
arcSegments = T  ·  turnRatio = 0.2 + (T × 0.02)
How your bot curves. High T = sharp tight turns. Low T = wide sweeping arcs.
Power — Charge & Follow-Through
charge = P²  ·  followThrough = P × 200 ms
Doubling your power stat quadruples your charge. The follow-through is how long the push lasts.
Budget Constraint
S + E + T + P = 20
All four stats must add up to exactly 20. No more, no less. Prove your loadout is legal.
Percent Change
% Change = (New − Old) ÷ Old × 100
How much a stat improved from Season 1 to Season 2. Shows your upgrade decision.
