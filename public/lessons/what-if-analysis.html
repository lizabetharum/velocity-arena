"Endurance is capped at 4 points for the Championship. Any team with Endurance above 4 must reallocate those extra points to Speed — immediately."

This means teams who spent a lot on Endurance just got a forced trade: more Speed, but weaker staying power. Your job is to figure out exactly what that math looks like — for every team in the league.

Before you start: two formulas to keep handy
Decay Rate = (20 − E) × 1.5
E = Endurance points  ·  Result = % power lost per minute  ·  Higher E = slower decay = bot lasts longer
Speed Motor Power = Speed × 5
Speed points × 5 = motor power as a percentage
Quick example: If E = 6, decay rate = (20 − 6) × 1.5 = 14 × 1.5 = 21% per minute. That means the bot loses 21% power every minute it runs.
