The Reveal

You've already seen the pattern. Now it gets a name.

Linear

Speed formula

Equal steps produce equal jumps. Every extra point of Speed adds exactly 5% motorPower, no matter where you start.

Quadratic

Power formula

Equal steps produce bigger and bigger jumps. Doubling Power points doesn't double the output — it quadruples it.

Speed (Linear)
motorPower = Speed × 5
Power (Quadratic)
chargeSpeed = Power²

Linear means equal steps → equal jumps (straight line). Quadratic means equal steps → bigger and bigger jumps (curve that steepens). Both formulas use the same stat points, but the shapes are completely different.
