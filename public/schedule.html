About the I Do / We Do / You Do structure
I Do
Teacher models the task with full narration. Students observe and ask questions. No student devices yet.
We Do
Teacher and students work through the task together. Students follow along on their devices. Pair discussion built in.
You Do
Students work independently or in pairs. Teacher circulates. Challenge problem for early finishers.
