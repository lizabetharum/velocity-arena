THE CHALLENGE

Three phases. Twenty days. No exceptions.

PHASE 01 · WEEK 1

Build & learn

Allocate four stats — Speed, Endurance, Turning, Power — that set the math behind every match. Day 1 you have parts. Day 5 you have a robot ready to play.

→BBC micro:bit v2 + Cutebot Pro
→Stat-allocation system (S+E+T+P=20)
→Coordinate-grid navigation
PHASE 02 · WEEKS 2–3

Program & play

MakeCode block coding. The robot does what you tell it. The math tells you what to say. Two seasons of round-robin matches with pre-match prediction and post-match residual analysis.

→MakeCode editor
→Live data dashboard, every team
→You vote rule changes between seasons
PHASE 03 · WEEK 4

Compete

Scouting reports. Pre-match pitches scored on mathematical specificity. Championship bracket. Community Exhibition where you teach the math to visitors. The arc ends with you as the authority.

→Championship + finals bracket
→Pre-match pitch to judges
→Best Teacher award (visitor-voted)
