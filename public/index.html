"You have 20 points. Four stats. One Championship. Figure out the best way to use them. Then prove it."
You are on a three-person team. Your team programs a robot to play soccer on a coordinate grid. Every team gets the same 20 points to split across four stats: Speed, Endurance, Turning, and Power. No one gets more. No one gets less.

Your job is to figure out which split wins. You will use math to predict what your robot will do. You will run matches to test the predictions. You will change the rules. You will compete.

By Day 20, you will be able to look at a formula, tell someone what it means, and show them on the field.
