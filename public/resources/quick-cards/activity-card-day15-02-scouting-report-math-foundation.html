Say Script
Launch Season 2 is over. You have four match days of data, a linear equation, and a record. The Scouting Report turns all of that into a competitive preparation tool. You are analyzing one assigned opponent. Four components. One worksheet per person. This is not a team document — every student produces their own findings.
Component 4 (Critical) Component 4 is the most important calculation in this block. Take your opponent's equation. Plug in your own current x value — your stat, not theirs. What does y equal? That is what their equation predicts will happen when they play you. Write that number. Now run your equation on their x value. Write that number. You have two predicted win probabilities. They are not going to agree. That disagreement is your strategy gap.
Share-Out Per team: What win probability does your opponent's equation predict against you? Write all numbers on board. Then: Which team has the largest gap between what their equation predicts and what the opponent's equation predicts?
Hard StopComponent 4 check at 25 min — both win probabilities written before any team moves on. Sections 1–2 due at end of Day 15 Open Lab. Collect for review.
