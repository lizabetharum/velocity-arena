Before Block Starts
  • Match field clear. All bots charging or at stations.
  • 3–4 round-robin pairings pre-assigned. Posted or ready to announce. (6 teams = up to 15 matchups; prioritize first 3–4 rounds.)
  • Dashboard open on every device: three completion fields — (1) goals per minute per match, (2) possession time by team, (3) W/L by opponent's dominant stat
  • Leaderboard visible — current standings before final matches begin
  • Loss-recovery log accessible: match #, opponent's winning stat, one sentence on specific cause
Dashboard Fields — What Each Means

Goals per min: total goals ÷ match duration. Normalizes for early stoppages.

Possession time: estimated seconds each team's bot controlled scoring zone. Estimate with (E) flag beats blank.

W/L by stat matchup: for each loss, identify opponent's dominant stat. Group losses by that stat. Tells teams exactly which stat is the Season 2 problem.

Students Produce
  • 3–4 data log rows + dashboard entries per round
  • Loss-recovery entry per loss (match #, opponent stat, specific cause sentence)
  • Full Season 1 dashboard: all 3 metrics, all matches, no blanks
  • Final leaderboard read + Season 1 standings

Round Call Script
Start "Bots to start position. Match starts in 30 seconds."
End "Time. Bots down. Record your result in the dashboard now."
Recovery "Losing teams — 4 minutes. Write: match number, opponent's dominant stat, one sentence on the specific reason you lost. Not 'we got outscored.' A specific cause."
Reset "Next round pairings on the board. Set up."
Loss Recovery — Valid Entry (3 Parts)
  1. Match number and opponent
  2. Opponent's winning stat: "They had Speed = 12."
  3. Specific cause: "Our Endurance = 3 meant our bot slowed to 16% power by minute 3. Their Speed advantage compounded our energy loss."

"We were slower" or "They were better at everything" = invalid. "Which stat? Which minute?" Require revision before next round.

Watch For
Losing team skips recovery to prep for next round. "You need your loss-recovery entry before you set up for round N+1. It's 4 minutes. Write it." Non-optional.
Winning team ignores data log. "What stat matchup advantage did you have? Enter it in the W/L by stat matchup column." Winning data is what losing teams study for Season 2.
Both bots malfunction simultaneously. Void match — do not log. Re-run once if time allows. If not, move to next pairing.
Dashboard entry only today's matches. "Your dashboard needs all Season 1 matches, not just today. Open your data log and fill in earlier rows."
No possession time data — never tracked. "Estimate from memory. Write 'E' in parentheses — an estimate with a note is more useful than a blank."
