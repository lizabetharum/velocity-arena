MATH: 5 questions: percent change · slope-intercept · distance formula · endurance zero-crossing · unit rate
Before Check Starts
  • Scrap paper or half-sheets at every seat. Nothing else on tables — worksheets, stat cards, devices away before launch.
  • All 5 questions written on board or projected. Campers read from board, not a handout.
  • Answer key in hand — you read aloud immediately at 0:10.
Questions (Write on Board)
  1. Two teams: Speed=6 and Speed=9. What is the percent change in motor power output?
  2. A line has slope 2 and passes through (1, 3). Write the equation in slope-intercept form.
  3. Calculate the distance from (2, 5) to (7, 17).
  4. P(t) = 100 − 12t. At what value of t does P reach zero?
  5. A bot travels 150 cm in 5 seconds. Rate in cm/s? Distance in 8 seconds at that rate?
Students Produce
  • Individual written answers to 5 questions — no notes, no devices
  • Q4 is the Season 2 planning diagnostic — flag blanks now
