Race Script
Say "Twenty cards. All six formula types, shuffled. One person flips the top card. Your team solves it together. When you have an answer and the work written, bring the card and your paper to me. I stamp correct work and you go back to flip the next card. Incorrect work comes back for one more attempt, then you move on."
Say "First team to finish all 20 with fewer than 3 errors wins. Your finish time goes on the board the moment you hand me your last card. Race ends at 9:47 regardless."
Say "After the race: you sort cards by formula type and count your errors. That count tells you something you need before this afternoon. Do not flip until I say go."
Stamp Station Rules
Correct answer, no work shown. "Show me the formula." Do not stamp until the substitution step is on paper.
Wrong formula, correct answer by coincidence. Do not stamp. "Show me the formula step." The formula must be right.
Partially shown work + correct answer. Stamp it.
Team stalls on one type at 15-min mark (<8 stamped). "You have 10 minutes. Move through what you can." That is the only intervention.
Returned card: "Check your work." Nothing else. Do not identify the error. Do not hint at the formula.
Hard Stop10:08 — Target types on board. Predictions collected. Transition → Lunch.
