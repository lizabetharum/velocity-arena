Pre-Match Ritual (Every Round)
  • Call both teams for the matchup
  • Each team states prediction number out loud — both teams hear both numbers
  • Confirm predictions are written in data log before match starts — wait if blank
  • Start all matches simultaneously on your signal
Students Produce

  • Dashboard updated with all round results
  • Loss-recovery: one hypothesis (what to change + why) per loss

Loss Recovery — Word for Word, in Order
Step 1 "That result was frustrating. That's a real feeling and it makes sense." Wait 30 seconds. Add nothing.
Step 2 "Your model predicted X goals. You scored Y. That gap is the most interesting thing that happened today. Let's find the one decision that created it." Open dashboard. One stat only.
Step 3 "You have N matches left. You now have data you didn't have before. What's one thing you'd change?" Write their answer. That is their next hypothesis. Do not evaluate it.
Step 4 "Walk to one other team. Tell them: 'We're changing _____ because _____.' One sentence. Then come back."
When Recovery Script Breaks Down
Camper in tears / shut down. Sit with them 30–60 sec. "We'll look at the dashboard in 5 minutes." Don't skip Step 2.
Team lost 4–5 consecutive matches. "Show me one match where your prediction was close. What was different?" Open Lab: one stat only.
Watch for: prediction inflation. "Your prediction goes in before you hear theirs. Write what your equation gave you."
