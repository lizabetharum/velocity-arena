Launch Script
Say "Every team passes their dashboard to a partner team — not your rival. They find one pattern you missed in 10 minutes. Outside analysts see what collectors can't. That's the exercise."
Say [Read pairings from board] "Pass your dashboard to your partner team now. You have 10 minutes to find one pattern in their data. Write it on the index card: 'We noticed [pattern]. This matters because [implication].' Go."
Finding Card Format

"We noticed [pattern]. This matters because [implication]."

Pattern = something specific: a number, a match, a stat relationship. Not a result. Implication = what it means for their next match or Season 2 planning.

Watch For
Finding is a result, not a pattern. "1-2 is a result. What in the data explains it? Which match was closest? Which stat?" Push to the number.
Team processing the swap finding instead of acting. "Track A or Track B. Your finding tells you which question to ask. Pick a track."
Do not tell teams what to adjust. "What surprised you in today's matches?" Wait. Do not suggest a stat to look at.
