Vote Rule
Proposing team does not vote on their own proposal.
Simple majority of all other teams = passes.
Example: 6 teams total. Team A presents. 5 remaining vote. Need ≥ 3 yes to pass.

Verbatim Scripts
Opening Each team presents for exactly 3 minutes: cite two data points, explain your rule, describe the impact on the last-place team. After each presentation the room checks the math, then votes — proposing team abstains, majority wins. Passed rules go on the wall immediately. First team: you're up.
Math Check (after every pres.) Does this proposal use the data correctly? Can anyone verify the math? Say this after EVERY presentation including the strongest one. Do not skip it.
If PASSED — write on wall Walk to wall. Write in permanent marker. Say: Written. Season 2 rule.
Objection Window Objection window open. 2 minutes. If you believe this rule disproportionately affects your team: state your team name, the specific number, and the calculation. Go. No number + calculation within 30 sec = close window.
If NOT PASSED Not enough votes. Next team. No discussion.
Post-Vote Revision Rules on the wall apply to every team. Revise your Season 2 allocation to satisfy all of them — total still sums to 20, 22 for the Underdog. No one leaves without a complete, rule-compliant sheet.
Hard Stop 2:00 — Collect every revised Season 2 allocation sheet before teams move to Open Lab. A team without a complete, rule-compliant sheet carries the error into Season 2 match programming.
