Loss Recovery — Step 2 (Day 9 Version)

Step 2 "Your prediction used [Formula Relay target type]. Your model said X goals. You scored Y. Look at one input: did the number you plugged into the formula match what you actually ran with today?" Ref the Formula Relay board. One variable — did the input match?
Step 3 "You have N matches left. The gap entry you write now is the one sentence your next prediction builds on. What one variable would you change?"
