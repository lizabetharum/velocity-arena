Launch Script (0:00–0:04)

Write team names on scoreboard while talking — don't wait until done speaking.

Say "Today is the first match day. Before your bot competes, you compete."
Say "Every person calculates independently — no talking, no sharing. When all three agree, all three stand. Right answer earns a shot at the bin. Made shot: bonus point."
Say "Four rules. One: your own paper first, then agree. Two: all three stand — one person sitting means no shot. Three: shooter rotates each round. Four: my count of the shot is final."
Watch For
Only one person calculating. Point to another member: "What's your answer?" If they shrug, the team does not stand.
Team stands without all members. Call it: "Everyone has to stand." They sit back down. Correct the problem, stand again if right.
Wrong answer accepted. Correct it immediately, in front of the room. "That's not right. Sit. Try again." No ambiguity.
Endurance calc errors (Round 2). P(3) = 100 − 17 × 1.5 × 3 = 23.5%. Common error: forgetting the (20 − E) subtraction first.
