Day 18 · Session 01
20 min · Opening
🏆 5 phases: acknowledgment → bracket read-aloud → strategy sentence (1 number required) → sportsmanship → final bot check
5 Ritual Phases
  • Phase 1 — Opening acknowledgment
  • Phase 2 — Bracket read-aloud (every team reads their matchup)
  • Phase 3 — Strategy sentence (one number required, written on board)
  • Phase 4 — Sportsmanship acknowledgment
  • Phase 5 — Final loadout check
You Need
  • Bracket posted and visible
  • Strategy sentence board space ready
  • Bots charged and at stations
Produces
  • Every team has stated a number-backed strategy
  • Sportsmanship norm re-established
  • Bots verified and ready
Sequence
0:00OpeningQuiet room. "Today is Championship. Everything from Day 1 leads here."
0:02Bracket readEach team reads their Round 1 matchup aloud: "We play ___ in Round 1."
0:07Strategy sentenceEach team writes one sentence on the board with one specific number. Example: "Our Speed is 72 — we win on distance." Read aloud, then discuss 2 min.
0:14SportsmanshipRe-read the agreed norm from early sessions. Brief acknowledgment — not a lecture.
0:17Final checkBots on, MakeCode values confirmed against loadout card. 3 min max.
0:20Championship beginsTransition to match stations. No further adjustments.
Say
Opening"You have been building this for 17 days. Every number in your scouting report came from work you did. Today you compete with it."
Strategy"One number. That is your strategy sentence requirement. If you cannot put a number in it, it is not a strategy — it is a hope."
Sports"Win with the same math you used to prepare. Dispute with numbers, not noise."
Watch For
Strategy sentence has no number — send back: "Add a specific value from your loadout or scouting report."
Bot values don't match loadout card — fix now — last chance before competition
Anxiety or shutdown before matches — "Your math is already done. You are not guessing — you are executing."
Hard Stop0:20 — every team has a number-backed strategy sentence, bots verified, Championship begins
