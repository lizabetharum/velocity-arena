Say Script
Launch 9:00 Season 2 starts today. Before anything else: one prediction per team. Write it on the index card in front of you. The format is on the board: "We will win X of 9 matches." One number. Not a range. You have 4 minutes. Do not look at other teams' cards before you write yours. Go.
Count of Three When I say three, all cards go up at the same time. Ready? One — two — three. Hold the count if anyone isn't ready.
After Posting Let room read silently for 30 sec. Then: One observation per team — something you notice about the predictions on the wall. Not a critique. Just what you see.
Data Check Per team: What Season 1 data shaped that number? Accept one specific data reference. Move on. Do not let one team's answer go longer than 30 sec.
Wrap 9:16 These cards stay up all week. This afternoon you will build a linear equation from your match data and use it to predict your win probability match by match. At the end of the week, you'll see how close your gut was to your math. Those two numbers are supposed to be different — that's how you know you learned something.
Hard Stop 9:20 — Transition to Energy Reset. Prediction cards must be posted before moving on — simultaneous post is the structural protection against anchoring. Do not skip the count of three.
