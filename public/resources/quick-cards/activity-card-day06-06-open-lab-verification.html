Open Lab: Free Code or Final Verification
6 of 6
Day 6 · Open Lab Block · 45 min
Follows Scoop Finalization
MATH: No new formula — verification only. Teams check code values against four stat formulas: Speed, Endurance, Turning, Power. Mismatches = PENDING.
Before You Start
  • Pull verification log from Scoop Finalization — know which teams have open mismatches before campers arrive
  • Print one Pre-Match Checklist per team, or confirm device access
  • MakeCode open and ready on all devices
  • Know your PENDING risk teams — reach them first
  • No split track. The checklist is the activity for everyone. State PENDING consequence at the open — not the close.
Students Produce
  • Verified formula values for all four stats (Steps 1–4 on checklist)
  • Reality check: three bot behaviors confirmed or flagged (Step 2)
  • Final lock status circled: Cleared or PENDING
Formula Reference
Speed Motor Power = S × 5
Common error: wrong multiplier or misread stat. Fix code, not stat.
Endurance Decay Rate = 20 − Endurance
Common error: subtraction mistakes. High decay = bot slows too fast.
Turning turnRatio = 0.2 + (Turning × 0.02)
Common error: updating one variable, forgetting Arc Segment.
Power Charge Power = Power²  ·  Follow Through × 200
Common error: squaring wrong value or skipping follow-through step.
Timing
Opening
2 min		 Send teams Deliver direction in one pass — goal, consequence, time window. Do not explain the checklist step by step. Send them. Circulate immediately.
Step 1 — Formula Verification (Speed · Endurance · Turning · Power)
Ongoing Circulate Move team to team. Confirm each stat check before they advance. Expected value vs. code value — exact match required. Do not wait for hands.
Ongoing Mismatch found Fix the code value — never the stat. Stats are locked from the Scoop Sheet. If a team changes the stat: revert immediately, log it.
Step 2 — Reality Check (3 min per team)
Ongoing Run the bot Three questions: correct speed? correct turning? slowing over time? One fail = return to that formula check. Do not skip this for teams who think they're done — it catches transcription errors formula review misses.
Step 3 — Final Lock
Ongoing Lock status Teams circle: "Our code matches our stats" or "We fixed mismatches." Either = Cleared. Cannot circle either = PENDING. Early finishers: free code, stat variables stay locked.
3:00 left Last call Check every team with open mismatches. If close, give the time. If not close: "You will be PENDING for tomorrow's first match until this is resolved." Do not wait for the close to say this.
Wrap
5 min		 Devices away Call devices at 5 min before end. Give every team a clear status before they leave. No one exits in an ambiguous state. Record every PENDING team in your log before leaving the room.
End Hard stop Every team must leave with a locked status — Cleared or PENDING. PENDING teams must be flagged for the start of the next session. Resolved before the first match block.
Say This
Open
"Every team is running the Pre-Match Checklist right now. Four stat checks, a quick reality check with your bot, then you lock your status. If your numbers and your code match, you're cleared. If they don't, fix it before you leave — any mismatch you don't resolve today is a PENDING on tomorrow's first match. Start with Speed."
Close
"Devices away. If your checklist is complete and your status is locked, you are cleared for tomorrow's match. If you have a mismatch you did not resolve, you are PENDING — we fix it before the first match tomorrow. See me on the way out if you are not sure of your status."
Not This
Don't negotiate the PENDING consequence. Don't let teams change stat values — only code values. Don't accept "I think it's right" without the calculation on paper. Don't skip the reality check for teams who claim they're already done.
Watch For → Redirect
Team circles YES on a check without calculating "Show me the math." If they can't, have them run the calculation on paper before moving on.
Team fixes the stat instead of the code "Stats are locked from your Scoop Sheet. Fix the code value, not the stat." Revert immediately if they've already changed it. Log the incident.
Reality check fails but formula checks all passed Transcription error — correct formula, wrong number entered. Have them re-read each code value against the checklist calculation. Also check the download.
Team says they're done but skipped reality check "Last step — run the bot for 30 seconds and answer the three questions on the sheet." Not optional.
PENDING team argues the consequence "I understand that's frustrating. The fix is straightforward and won't take long. We do it before the first match tomorrow." Do not negotiate.
Team still has MakeCode open at session end "Device away. Session is done." Say it once. Do not repeat.
Hard Stop Every team leaves with a locked status — Cleared or PENDING. PENDING teams flagged in your log before you leave the room. Resolved before the first match block tomorrow.
