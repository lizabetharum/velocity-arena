PURPOSE: Match rules contract — 8 min speaking, 2 min questions · 5 rules, one sentence each · Today is practice, nothing counts in Season 1
Before You Start
  • Field set up: flat surface, two end lines taped, ball at midfield
  • Both bots at starting positions — visible while you talk
  • Timer ready to start on your signal
  • Data dashboards open on all devices
  • Do not display Season 1 standings or brackets. Today is practice only.
Students Produce
  • Confirmed understanding of 5 match rules
  • Questions asked and answered before first simulation match

Timing

Say This
Duration
"A match is 3 minutes. I run the timer. When it hits zero, I call stop. Whatever the score is at that moment is the score."
Simultaneous
"Your bots will collide. That is not a foul. That is the game."
No touching
"Once the match starts, you do not touch your bot and you do not change your code. If your bot gets stuck, it stays stuck. That is your data."
Record first
"The moment a match ends, both teams record the score in their data dashboard. Score first, everything else second. No exceptions."
Not this
This is a contract, not a strategy session. If you run over 8 minutes of speaking, you're explaining too much. Each rule is one sentence.
