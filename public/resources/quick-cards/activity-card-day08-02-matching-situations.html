Misconception Correction Script (0:46–0:50)

Deliver from the board. Do not skip or abbreviate.

Say "Someone looks at the scatter plot and thinks: my Speed is 10 — if Speed doubles to 20, my goals should double too. Let's check that."
Say "Look at your scatter plot. Find Speed = 5. Find Speed = 10. Are the goals exactly double?" Pause 20 seconds.
Say "Usually no. The line doesn't go through the origin unless b = 0. There's a y-intercept. There's variability. Read the actual numbers before you assume doubling works."
Board Write: Proportional: y = kx → doubles when x doubles / Linear + intercept: y = mx + b → does NOT double when x doubles
Say "Label these two in your worksheet notes. This comes back in Season 2."
