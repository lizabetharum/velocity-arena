Lobby Rules (Post)
#Rule
1Conversation, not presentation — no slides, no scripts, no standing at front
2Talk to at least two other teams in 10 minutes — move, don't stay with one team
3Feedback is optional to use — your proposal stays yours
4Prepare for objections — start calculating percent change if you hear a rule that hurts your team
Students Produce
  • One or two targeted revisions to proposal (not a full rewrite)
  • Proposal locked by 9:25 — no further changes
