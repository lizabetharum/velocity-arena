Conditional Simon Says
1 of 7
20 Min · Hook Block · Day 5 · AM
Opening Activity
Before You Start
  • Campers have their stat cards — all four stats filled in
  • Space to stand and move; chairs can stay at tables
  • Don't explain the math connection before Round 5. Let the reveal land on its own.
Students Produce
  • Physical response to 5 conditional rounds
  • Heard and responded to the bridge question
  • Saw the equation S + P + T + E = 20 written on the board

Budget
Round 5: everyone cheers. Wait a full beat. Then: "Why did everyone just cheer?" Write the equation on the board only after they answer.
Timing
0–3Rounds 1–2 R1: equality check, one variable. "Why did you stand? / Why didn't you?" R2: inequality — give campers a beat longer to check. Both correct outcomes are valid.
3–8Rounds 3–4 R3: two-variable sum. "How many of you have S + E = 10?" Count hands. Bridge: "What would have to change for your hand to go up?" Don't answer it. Move on.
8–12Round 5 "IF S + P + T + E = 20, THEN cheer." Everyone cheers. Wait a full beat. Go quiet. Ask: "Why did everyone just cheer?" Take 2 answers.
12–15Reveal Write on board: Speed + Power + Turning + Endurance = 20. "That is the equation we're working with today. You just proved it with your hands." Then Two-Formula Warm-Up.
15–20Bridge Q Return to the Round 3 bridge question: "What would have to change about your allocation for your hand to go up?" One camper who answers it has solved for a variable — name that.
Say This
R1
"Simon Says — IF your Speed equals 5, THEN stand up."
R3 bridge
"What would have to change about your allocation for your hand to go up next time?"
R5 reveal
"Exactly. And that —" [write equation] "— is the equation we're working with today. You just proved it with your hands."
Not this
Don't answer the bridge question. Let it sit. Campers who answer it have already solved for a variable without knowing it.
Watch For → Redirect
Acts on every condition "Simon Says is conditional. Read your card — does the condition apply to you?" Stop the round.
Doesn't act when they should After the round: "Did the condition apply to you? What does your card say?"
R3: no one claps Valid outcome — it means no one's S + E = 10. Acknowledge it and use the bridge question.
Camper argues R5 doesn't count "Everyone's stats sum to 20 — that was the constraint since Day 1. Everyone qualifies."
