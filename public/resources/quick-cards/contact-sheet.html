BContact sheet

Day 01 contact sheet — print proof

What you see before you commit a tree to the printer. Each card is a half-scale 11×8.5 landscape sheet, in run-order. Prints in pure B&W on classroom toner; kind reads from the corner glyph (◐ ▣ ▲ ◇) and a black tag, and the bar under the header shows where this card lives in the day's run.

ONE SHEET = ONE CARD

Each card is a full 11×8.5 page

No cutting, no trimming. Print, clip together by day, drop on the clipboard. The N-of-8 in each header is the only registration you need — a stray sheet finds its way home from sequence number alone.

B&W BY DESIGN

Glyph + tag, not color

Cards print on classroom toner — no color budget. The kind reads from a typographic mark in the corner (◐ warm-up · ▣ discuss · ▲ build · ◇ reflect) plus a black inverted tag. Color stays in the week view, where screens live.

RUN-ORDER CLOCK

Where am I in the day?

Every sheet carries a black bar showing its slot in the day's total minutes. A teacher mid-lesson can glance down and know exactly how much runway is left — without recomputing from the agenda.
