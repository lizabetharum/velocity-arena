Launch 2:00 You walked out of yesterday's matches with evidence. This Open Lab is 35 minutes to turn that evidence into one concrete adjustment. Two tracks: Track A is residual analysis — you change one thing in your equation. Track B is bot calibration — you change one parameter in MakeCode. I'm assigning tracks based on what I saw yesterday. [Name team] — Track A. [Name team] — Track B. Route every team by name. Do not ask for preferences.
One Thing Rule One change. Not a redesign. If you change two things and your result improves, you won't know which one worked. Pick the one that matters most.
Track A Step Check What's your biggest residual from yesterday? Write that number first. Then tell me which direction — over or under? Then we talk about what to change.
Track B Step Check Before opening MakeCode: What specifically did the bot do that didn't match the allocation? Write that in one sentence. That's your diagnosis. Now open MakeCode and change the one parameter that addresses it.
Report-Out 2:32 Per team: One sentence — what did you change, and what is your updated prediction for today's matches? A number is required. "We improved it" is not a prediction.
