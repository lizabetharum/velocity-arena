Differentiation — Day 3
Support
Provide seed numbers — do not randomize
Use physical number cards for x, y, z
Pair with equation reference card
Focus on I Do and We Do only
Skip mod — just use the index directly
On track
All three tabs independently
Complete the challenge problem
Add 2+ words to each list
Explain coefficient difference to a partner
Extension
Change a coefficient — predict the effect first
Write a new equation for the animal
What list size would make mod irrelevant?
Graph a = 2x + y − 1 for fixed y
Discussion prompts
"If x goes up by 1, what happens to the adjective index? Why does x have more impact than y?"
"We have 26 adjectives but the equation can give us a number bigger than 25. How does the app handle that?"
"If you added 10 more animals to the list, would the same sliders give you the same animal? Why not?"
"What's the biggest team number you can get? What x and z would produce it?"
Timing notes
If warm-up runs long, cut it to one student response instead of two.
I Do can be shortened if the class is tracking well.
You Do needs at least 20 minutes — cut share-out to 2 minutes if needed, not You Do.
The challenge problem is optional. Do not cut the share-out entirely.
